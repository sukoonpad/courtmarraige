Arya Samaj Marriage Kya Hoti Hai?

Frequently Asked Questions

Arya Samaj marriage kitne time mein hoti hai?
Poora process sirf 1-2 ghante mein complete hota hai. Sab kuch — hawan, ceremony, aur certificate — ek hi din mein.
Kya family ko notice milta hai?
Bilkul nahi! Arya Samaj marriage mein koi notice period nahi hota aur kisi ko bhi koi notice nahi bheja jata. Ye poora process completely private hai.
Kya Muslim ya Christian Arya Samaj marriage kar sakte hain?
Nahi. Arya Samaj marriage only for Hindus, Sikhs, Jains, aur Buddhists. Inter-religion couples ke liye Special Marriage Act Court Marriage better option hai. Call karein details ke liye.
Arya Samaj certificate kahan kahan valid hai?
Ye certificate India mein har jagah valid hai — bank, passport office, embassy, property registration, insurance, sab jagah. Ye Delhi Government ka official document hai.
