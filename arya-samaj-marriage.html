Arya Samaj Marriage Eligibility

Arya Samaj Marriage is applicable for:

  • Hindu couples — both bride and groom should be Hindu
  • Sikh, Jain, Buddhist — these are also considered Hindu under Hindu Marriage Act 1955
  • Age: Groom minimum 21, Bride minimum 18 years
  • Unmarried or divorced with valid decree
  • Not in prohibited blood relation
  • Sound mind & mutual consent

Agar aap inter-religion couple hain (e.g. Hindu-Muslim, Hindu-Christian), toh Arya Samaj nahi — Special Marriage Act court marriage ya Inter-caste marriage service lein.

Arya Samaj Marriage Certificate — Legal Validity

Bahut log ye sochte hain ki Arya Samaj certificate legal nahi hai — yeh bilkul galat hai. Supreme Court of India ne multiple judgments mein confirm kiya hai ki Arya Samaj marriage Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 ke tahat legally valid hai.

Arya Samaj certificate ka use aap kar sakte hain:

  • Passport application mein spouse name addition ke liye
  • Bank mein joint account open karne ke liye
  • Insurance nominee update karne ke liye
  • Visa application mein spouse relationship prove karne ke liye
  • Government records (Aadhaar update etc.) ke liye

Agar aapko extra security chahiye, toh Arya Samaj ke baad aap Marriage Registration office mein certificate bhi register karwa sakte hain. Hum ye additional service bhi provide karte hain.
