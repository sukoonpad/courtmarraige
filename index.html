Court marriage ke liye kya umar chahiye?
Dulhe ki umar minimum 21 saal aur dulhan ki umar minimum 18 saal honi chahiye. Ye Government of India ka rule hai — dono ke liye date of birth proof zaroori hai (10th certificate, passport, ya birth certificate).
Kya alag religion ke log court marriage kar sakte hain?
Haan! Special Marriage Act, 1954 ke tahat koi bhi do vyakti — chahe Hindu-Muslim, Hindu-Christian, ya koi bhi religion — court marriage kar sakte hain. Ye India ki sabse flexible marriage act hai.
Court marriage mein kitna time lagta hai?
Arya Samaj Marriage: Sirf 1-2 ghante mein certificate. Court Marriage (Hindu Act): 24-48 ghante. Special Marriage Act: 30 din ka notice period hota hai. Tatkal Service: Same day possible hai extra charges mein.
Kya ghar walon ko pata chalega?
Arya Samaj marriage mein koi notice nahi bheja jata — poora process private rahta hai. Special Marriage Act mein 30 din ka public notice hota hai. Agar privacy chahiye toh Arya Samaj option sabse best hai.
Kya witnesses khud laane padte hain?
Nahi! Agar aapke paas witnesses nahi hain toh hum unhe arrange kar dete hain. Aapko sirf documents laane hain. Arya Samaj mein 2 witnesses aur Special Marriage Act mein 3 witnesses chahiye.
Kya NRI ya foreigner court marriage kar sakte hain?
Haan! NRI aur foreign nationals ke liye bhi court marriage possible hai. Embassy NOC guidance, visa support documentation, aur certificate courier service sab available hai. Call karein — hum sab handle karte hain.
Kya police protection milti hai?
Haan! Agar family opposition ho toh hum police protection arrange kar sakte hain court marriage ke waqt. Ye aapka legal right hai — Article 21 of Indian Constitution ke under. Call karein details ke liye.
Court marriage ka certificate kitne kaam aata hai?
Court marriage certificate bahut zaroori document hai: Visa aur passport mein spouse ke liye, bank accounts mein nominee, property registration, insurance claims, embassy applications, aur har jagah legal proof ke liye. Ye worldwide valid document hai.
