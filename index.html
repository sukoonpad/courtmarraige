Court marriage ke liye kya umar chahiye?
Dulhe ki umar minimum 21 saal aur dulhan ki umar minimum 18 saal honi chahiye. Dono ke liye DOB proof zaroori hai (10th certificate, passport, ya birth certificate).
Kya alag religion ke log court marriage kar sakte hain?
Haan! Special Marriage Act, 1954 ke tahat koi bhi do vyakti — chahe Hindu-Muslim, Hindu-Christian, ya koi bhi religion — court marriage kar sakte hain.
Rohini Court mein court marriage kaise karein?

Kya ghar walon ko pata chalega?
Arya Samaj marriage mein koi notice nahi bheja jata — poora process 100% private. Special Marriage Act mein 30 din ka public notice hota hai. Privacy ke liye Arya Samaj best hai.
Kya witnesses khud laane padte hain?
Nahi! Agar aapke paas witnesses nahi hain toh hum arrange kar dete hain free mein. Arya Samaj mein 2 witnesses aur Special Marriage Act mein 3 witnesses chahiye.
Kya police protection milti hai love marriage mein?
Haan! Family opposition ho toh hum police protection arrange kar sakte hain. Ye aapka legal right hai — Article 21, Indian Constitution. Expert legal support available hai.
