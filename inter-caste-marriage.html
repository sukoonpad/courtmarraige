Special Marriage Act 1954 — Aapka Legal Right

Special Marriage Act (SMA) India ka secular marriage law hai — isme religion, caste, community, ya community koi matter nahi karti. Ek Hindu ladka ek Muslim ladki se, ek Brahmin ladki ek Dalit ladke se, ek Indian aur ek foreign national — sab SMA ke tahat shaadi kar sakte hain bina religion badle.

Supreme Court ne baar baar confirm kiya hai (Lata Singh v. State of UP, 2006; Shakti Vahini v. Union of India, 2018) ki adult couples ko apna life partner choose karne ka constitutional right hai — Article 21 ke tahat. Koi bhi family, khap panchayat ya community group aapko force nahi kar sakti. Agar koi threat kare to police ki duty hai ki protection provide kare.

Eligibility — Inter-Caste / Inter-Religion Marriage

SMA ke tahat marriage ke liye basic conditions simple hain — religion ka koi bandhan nahi:

  • Umar — Ladka 21+ years, Ladki 18+ years
  • Mental soundness — dono partners mentally capable ho
  • Dono single ho — pehli shaadi legally khatm ho (divorce / death) to possible hai
  • Prohibited relationship mein na ho (blood relation / close family)
  • At least one partner Delhi mein 30 din se reside kar raha ho
  • Koi religion conversion zaroori nahi — dono apne apne religion mein reh sakte hain
