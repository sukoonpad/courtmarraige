Kaunse Acts Ke Tahat Register Kar Sakte Hain?

Aapki religion aur situation ke hisaab se alag acts apply hoti hain. Hum sab ke liye available hain.

Hindu Marriage Act, 1955

Hindu, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist couples ke liye. Arya Samaj ceremony ke baad ya seedha registration. Same day certificate possible.

⚡ Same Day Available

Special Marriage Act, 1954

Any religion ke couples ke liye. Inter-religion couples ke liye best. 30 din notice period (tatkal option available).

🌍 All Religions

Muslim Nikah Registration

Nikah ke baad government registration. 2 Muslim witnesses required. No notice sent to address. Same day process.

🔒 Confidential

Christian Marriage Registration

Church marriage ke baad government registration. Indian Christian Marriage Act, 1872 ke tahat.

Aksar Pooche Jawale

Pehle se shadi ho gayi — ab certificate kaise lein?
Agar aapki shadi pehle ho chuki hai (religious ya civil), toh bhi aap marriage registration karwa sakte hain. Affidavit ke saath application deni hogi. Hum sab handle karte hain — call karein.
Marriage certificate banwane mein kitna time lagta hai?
Tatkal service mein 24 ghante mein, Arya Samaj ke baad same day, Hindu Marriage Act mein 2-3 din, aur Special Marriage Act mein 30-40 din (tatkal option available).
Kya certificate online bana sakte hain?
Application online ho sakti hai, lekin physical presence zaroori hai registrar office mein. Hum aapko step by step guide karenge — call ya WhatsApp karein.
