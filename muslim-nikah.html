Meher — Documentation aur Legal Importance

Meher (dower) ek compulsory gift hai jo dulha apni dulhan ko Nikah ke time deta hai. Yeh Islamic law mein bride ka haq (right) hai aur Nikah-Nama mein clearly mention hona zaroori hai. Hamare packages mein Meher documentation include hai.

Definition
Meher Kya Hai?

Meher woh amount ya property hai jo dulha apni dulhan ko Nikah ke waqt ya baad mein dene ka vaada karta hai. Yeh bride ka exclusive right hai — isme koi share nahi hota.

Types
Prompt vs Deferred Meher

Prompt Meher Nikah ke time ya demand par diya jaata hai. Deferred Meher kisi specific time ya condition par diya jaata hai. Dono Nikah-Nama mein document karna zaroori hai.

Legal Status
Court Mein Enforceable

Indian courts mein Meher ek legally enforceable debt hai. Agar dulha Meher nahi deta toh dulhan Indian courts se recovery kar sakti hai. Proper documentation essential hai.

Amount
Meher Ki Raqam

Meher ki koi fixed minimum ya maximum amount nahi hai. Dono families aapsi raazi se decide karte hain. Kuch log cash dete hain, kuch gold ya property.

Documentation
Nikah-Nama Mein Entry

Hamare Kazi Meher ki raqam, type (prompt/deferred) aur payment condition Nikah-Nama mein clearly likhte hain. Dono parties aur witnesses ke saath signed.

Why Important
Bride Ka Financial Security

Meher bride ko financial security deta hai. Divorce ya talaq ki situation mein Meher pehli priority hai. Proper documentation se future disputes avoid hote hain.
