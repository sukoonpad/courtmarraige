Nikah Eligibility — Kaun Kar Sakta Hai?

Muslim Nikah karne ke liye Special Marriage Act se alag eligibility rules hain. Basic conditions yeh hain:

  • Dono partners Muslim hone chahiye (Sunni / Shia / any sect).
  • Umar — Ladka 21+, Ladki 18+ (Indian law ke hisaab se recommended).
  • Dono mentally sound aur willing hone chahiye — Ijab-o-Qubool without coercion.
  • 2 Muslim witnesses (gawah) present ho — ek lady ko 2 lady witnesses bhi consider ki jaati hain.
  • Mehr (dower) amount mutually decide ki jaati hai aur Nikahnama mein likhi jaati hai.
  • Dono pehli shaadi se free ho (widow/divorced ho to iddat period complete ho).

Agar ek partner Muslim hai aur dusra non-Muslim, to Nikah directly nahi ho sakta — is case mein Special Marriage Act (Inter-Religion) ka rasta lena padta hai. Confusion ho to humse direct baat karein.

Nikah ke liye Zaruri Documents

Nikah ceremony ke din bas yeh documents laane honge — humari complete service mein drafting, affidavit aur photo arrangement humare paas hi hai.

Documents Checklist (Dono Partners)

  • Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy)
  • Age proof — 10th Marksheet / Birth Certificate / Passport
  • 4–6 Passport size photographs (each)
  • Residence proof — Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport
  • Religion proof — not mandatory but helpful (any document showing Muslim identity)
  • Widow/Divorcee case mein — death certificate / divorce decree
  • 2 Muslim witnesses ka Aadhaar (agar apne laa rahe ho — warna humare paas arranged hain)

Mehr ki amount couple decide karta hai — yeh token ya significant ho sakta hai. Nikahnama ke time yeh amount clearly mention ki jaati hai.

Frequently Asked Questions — Muslim Nikah Delhi

Kya Nikah ke liye bride's family ki permission zaroori hai?
Islamic law ke hisaab se — bride baaligh (adult) hai aur willingly consent de rahi hai, to Nikah valid hai. Hum 100% privacy maintain karte hain — family ko inform karna couple ki marzi par hai. No notice sent.
Nikah mein kitna time lagta hai?
1-2 ghante — documents verify hone se lekar Nikahnama hand-over tak. Same day complete. Appointment 24 ghante advance book karwa lein.
Mehr kitna rakhna chahiye?
Mehr couple ki mutual agreement par depend karta hai — minimum token amount (₹786, ₹1,001 etc.) se lekar substantial amount tak — jaise bhi dono decide karein. Nikahnama mein clearly written hota hai.
Kya Nikahnama se passport ban sakta hai?
Nikahnama legally valid hai, lekin passport / visa / foreign embassy ke liye court-registered marriage certificate highly recommended hai. Hum dono ek saath arrange kar sakte hain — combo pricing.
Inter-religion Nikah ho sakti hai (Muslim + non-Muslim)?
Inter-religion mein traditional Nikah legally complex hai. Aise cases mein hum Special Marriage Act route recommend karte hain — yeh har religion ke liye valid hai.
